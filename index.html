Transportation notice:

 
The last person to board the bus should always do a final sweep to see if there is anyone left planning to board the bus. If there isn't you can tell the driver you are the last one, otherwise, the person who is left is then responsible for the same sweep protocol. We had a student left at the SAU on Friday by mistake. We don't want that happening again.
